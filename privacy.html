Privacy Policy

Last updated 2 Mar, 2025

Types of Data we Collect

To provide our services effectively and maintain the integrity of our platform, we collect and process certain types of data. Our data collection is guided by principles of transparency, necessity, and security.

Account and Platform Data

  • User Registration Information
    • Email address: Used for account verification, required for communications, encrypted storage, retained for account duration
    • Discord ID: Service integration, support communications, community features
    • Nametag: Public identification, community interaction, user recognition
  • Authentication Data
    • IP Addresses: Login security, fraud prevention, 90-day retention
    • User-Agent: Browser identification, system compatibility, security verification

Service Usage Data

  • File Processing Information
    • File Metadata: File size, duration, format type, processing time
    • Processing Statistics: Success rates, error logs, performance metrics
    • Quality Metrics: Transcription accuracy, confidence scores
  • Platform Interaction
    • Usage Patterns: Feature utilization, session duration, frequency of use
    • Error Reports: Crash analytics, performance issues, bug reports
    • Preferences: User settings, theme choices, language preferences

Data Usage and Processing

Service Provision

Your data enables us to deliver and improve our audio/video transcription services:

  • Core Functionality
    • Audio/video file processing and transcription
    • User account management and authentication
    • Service personalization and preference storage
    • Performance optimization and quality assurance

Communication

We use your contact information for essential communications:

  • Service Notifications
    • Processing completion alerts
    • Service updates and maintenance notices
    • Security alerts and important changes
Note: We never sell your personal data to third parties. All data processing complies with applicable privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

Data Security

Protection Measures

  • Encryption
    • End-to-end Encryption: All file transfers use TLS 1.3 encryption
    • Data at Rest: AES-256 encryption for stored files and databases
    • Secure Key Management: Industry-standard key rotation and management
  • Access Control
    • Role-based Access: Strict permission levels for team members
    • Multi-factor Authentication: Required for all administrative access
    • Audit Logging: Comprehensive access and modification tracking

Data Retention

  • Processed Files
    • Original Media Files: Automatically deleted 24 hours after processing
    • Transcription Results: Retained for 30 days unless manually saved
    • Backup Copies: Emergency backups retained for 7 days maximum
  • Account Data
    • Active Accounts: Retained while account remains active
    • Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 12 months of inactivity
    • Account Deletion: Complete data purge within 30 days of request

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal data:

  • Access and Control
    • Right to access all personal data we hold about you
    • Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
    • Right to delete your account and associated data
    • Right to export your data in machine-readable format
    • Right to object to specific data processing activities
To exercise any of these rights, please contact our privacy team at privacy@conv.example.com. We respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.
